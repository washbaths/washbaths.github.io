Washington Baths





Nov 17, 2023

In solidarity with the International Call for a Global Shutdown for Palestine
Washington Baths will be closed today.

We cannot simply continue on with business as usual.
We must recognize the atrocities and war crimes that are being committed
with active complicity from our elected leaders, using our weapons,
with our security and defense as justification.
We must stop.

We are demanding an immediate ceasefire in Gaza,
and an end to the occupation and apartheid in the
Occupied Palestinian Territories.
